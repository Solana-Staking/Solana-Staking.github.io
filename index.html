What is Solana Staking?

Solana staking is the network's native SOL delegation flow: holders assign stake to validators while retaining control of their tokens. The official Solana staking FAQ explains validator selection, rewards, activation, and withdrawal timing.

How Solana Staking works

  1. Open official resourcesStart from solana.com and use a wallet that supports stake operations.
  2. Create a stake accountMove the SOL you want to delegate into a stake account, keeping some SOL liquid for network fees.
  3. Select a validatorReview commission, identity, uptime, and concentration; the Solana Foundation does not recommend a particular validator.
  4. Delegate and monitorSubmit the delegation in your wallet, then track activating, active, deactivating, or inactive status.

Before you stake

Control stays with the holder

Delegation gives a validator voting weight, not ownership or control of your SOL.

Rewards are variable

Yield depends on network inflation, total active stake, validator uptime, and commission, so avoid pages promising a fixed return.

Status is visible onchain

Once you have a stake account address, use Solana Explorer to review activation, delegation, and authority details.

Solana Staking FAQ

How does solana staking work?

You delegate SOL from a stake account to a validator that participates in consensus; delegation does not transfer ownership of the tokens.

Do validators control delegated SOL?

No. Native delegation gives the validator voting weight, while the wallet and stake authorities remain the control path for the holder.

When does a stake account become active?

Activation and deactivation happen around epoch boundaries. Solana describes an epoch as approximately 2 days, though network-wide limits can extend timing.

How are rewards and commission handled?

Rewards are issued to delegated stake accounts, and validator commission is taken as a percentage of earned rewards; outcomes vary by validator and network conditions.

Where can I verify stake status?

Use your wallet or paste the stake account address into Solana Explorer to inspect activation, delegation, and authority details.
